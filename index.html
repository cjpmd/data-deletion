CAMAI - Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

We take your privacy seriously and provide multiple ways to delete your data:

Option 1: Through Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings
  2. Navigate to Settings & Privacy > Settings
  3. Click on "Apps and Websites"
  4. Find "CAMAI" in your list of connected apps
  5. Click "Remove" to disconnect the app and request data deletion

Option 2: Direct Request

You can email us at cmcdonald002@dundee.ac.uk with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This process will:
  • Remove all your personal information
  • Delete your social media connections
  • Remove your content and preferences
  • Deactivate your account

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once completed.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at cmcdonald002@dundee.ac.uk.
